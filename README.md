## About Me

I'm a graduate student pursuing an M.S. in Computer Information Sciences with a
Cybersecurity concentration at Harrisburg University of Science and Technology. I

Technology and a B.A. in Computer Science from Hanover College.

My writing bridges the gap between complex technical systems and the people who
need to understand and act on them — whether that's a first-time Substack creator,

architecture frameworks.
