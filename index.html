About

Background

I'm a graduate student pursuing an M.S. in Computer Information Sciences with a Cybersecurity concentration at Harrisburg University of Science and Technology.

My writing bridges the gap between complex technical systems and the people who need to understand and act on them — whether that's a first-time Substack creator, a security analyst reviewing threat documentation, or an enterprise team evaluating architecture frameworks.

I specialize in security architecture, GRC frameworks, infrastructure design, and making technical concepts accessible without dumbing them down.
